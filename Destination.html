1. Choose Destination

Lalibela

UNESCO World Heritage site famous for its rock-hewn churches and religious history.

Activities:

  • Visit rock-hewn churches
  • Attend religious ceremonies
  • Explore local culture & history

Best Time to Visit: October – March

Axum

Ancient city known for obelisks and the legendary Queen of Sheba.

Activities:

  • Explore ancient obelisks
  • Visit museums
  • Discover archaeological ruins

Best Time to Visit: September – April

Simien Mountains

Stunning mountains with deep valleys and rare wildlife.

Activities:

  • Mountain trekking
  • Wildlife watching
  • Photography

Best Time to Visit: September – April

Harar

Historic walled city famous for markets and hyena feeding tradition.

Activities:

  • Walk inside the old city walls
  • Visit colorful markets
  • Watch hyena feeding at night

Best Time to Visit: October – March

Gondar

Known as the “Camelot of Africa”, Gondar is famous for its royal castles and rich imperial history.

Activities:

  • Visit Fasil Ghebbi castles
  • Explore ancient churches
  • Learn Ethiopian imperial history

Best Time to Visit: October – March

Afar / Danakil Depression

One of the hottest places on Earth, famous for lava lakes, salt flats, and colorful landscapes.

Activities:

  • Visit Erta Ale volcano
  • Explore salt flats
  • Experience Afar culture

Best Time to Visit: October – February

National Museum of Ethiopia

Home of the famous fossil “Lucy” and collections of Ethiopian history and culture.

Activities:

  • See ancient fossils and artifacts
  • Learn Ethiopian history
  • Explore cultural exhibitions

Best Time to Visit: Year-round

Omo Valley

Remote region famous for diverse tribes, traditions, and beautiful river landscapes.

Activities:

  • Visit indigenous tribal villages
  • Attend cultural festivals
  • Explore Omo River scenery

Best Time to Visit: November – March
